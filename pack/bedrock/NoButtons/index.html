Do you play Minecraft on your phone and don't like seeing those huge buttons on your screen like the control buttons?
This pack simply removes them!
You can also change the look of the bar at the top with the emote and the chat buttons.

There are 3 versions that you can switch between in the pack!

- Full (No buttons at all. Just the Hotbar)

- Default

- And Outlined (Just like default but with outlined buttons at the top)

I hope you like the pack, enjoy!
