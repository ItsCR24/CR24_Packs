Farming marks is a Resource Pack made by CR24 that allows you to see if your crops are at their max stage.

This pack has a green and red version of the outlines. You can choose them in the subpacks menu in the resource pack settings.

This pack allows you to see if your crops are ready to be farmed without confusion.
